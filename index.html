How to Use

  • Enter your name and click "Start Test"

  • Type the displayed text as accurately as possible

  • Progress through 20 levels with increasing difficulty

Scoring System

  • WPM: Words Per Minute (5 characters = 1 word)
  • Accuracy: Percentage of correct characters
  • Levels: 1-20 with 30 character increments
  • Mistakes: Incorrect characters are highlighted in red
